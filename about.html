About Curiosity

The Curiosity Project is a project created by Jiro Segoe on October 12th 2024.


The main purpose of the project is to create a machine that can create life to bring the Inanimate Insanity contestants into the world.


The Inanimate Insanity characters has been brought to life and came out of the Curiosity Recovery Center on November 9th 2024.


On January 2nd 2025, Jiro had the idea of creating the Toons from Dandy's World with this machine. He also creates the Toons days after their release on their original game.


Some day in March, he decides to also create Toons from an AU called "Candy's Bootleg World" which is a Dandy's World AU with bootleg Toons in a building near Gardenview. The reason of doing this is that these Toons are abused by the workers of that building.
